Butter Chicken

Home

Description

a savoury and filling dish with chicken, tomato, ginger, garlic, butter and cream.

the secret must have ingredient "kasoori methi" awakens Alladin in your kitchen

Ingredients

  • 50g garlic
  • 80g ginger
  • 1 tea spoon chili powder
  • 700g chicken breast
  • 2 spoons lemon juice
  • salt
  • 100g yoghurt
  • 1 tea spoon kasoori methi
  • 1 tea spoon mustard oil
  • 1 heaping spoon ginger garlic paste
  • 4 spoons butter
  • 2 onions
  • 1 spoon sugar
  • 1 tea spoon garam masala
  • 0.5 tea spoon cinnamon
  • 0.25 tea spoon cardamom
  • 2 gloves
  • 1 spoon ginger garlic paste
  • 650ml canned tomatoes
  • 1 spoon paprika powder
  • 1 spoon kasoori methi
  • 100ml heavy whipping cream
  • salt & pepper
  • 3 spoons cashew paste
  1. Dice the chicken breast fillets and mix them well with the other ingredients. Marinate for 20 minutes.
  2. Mix all second marinade ingredients thoroughly with the chicken and marinate for several hours, preferably overnight.
  3. Cook the marinated chicken by pan-frying, grilling, or roasting. (While a tandoori oven is ideal, a good pan or grill will also work). Set chicken aside.
  4. Heat half of the butter in a pot or pan and caramelize the onions with sugar for 5-10 minutes over medium heat until golden brown.
  5. Add cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves and roast for a few minutes.
  6. Add remaining butter and ginger-garlic paste and briefly roast together.
  7. Add tomatoes and paprika powder and cook for 20 minutes.
  8. Blend everything until completely smooth.
  9. Add kasoori methi, cream, chicken, pepper/salt, optionally blended nuts, and some water if desired. Simmer gently for 15 minutes.
  10. The sauce should be thick and abundant. (It's almost impossible to have too much sauce as it tastes so good!).
  11. Finally season the delicious dish with pepper/salt and chili powder.
